Silk Road (marketplace)

The Silk Road was an online marketplace launched in 2011 by Ross Ulbricht, operating on the dark web. It gained notoriety for facilitating the sale of illegal goods and services, primarily drugs, using the cryptocurrency Bitcoin for transactions.

AlphaBay (marketplace)

AlphaBay was launched in 2014 and operated on the Tor network, providing anonymity to users. It gained popularity among buyers and sellers looking to trade in illicit goods, including drugs, weapons, stolen data, and counterfeit items
