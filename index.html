About

🌟 My Journey:
What started as a curiosity became a calling when NYC public schools were hacked in 2022. My personal information was stolen in that breach, and that experience made cybersecurity feel real and urgent. Seeing how vulnerable our systems could be and being directly affected ignited a drive in me to understand how these attacks happen and how they can be prevented. Since then, I’ve been fully immersed in learning everything I can. Each challenge I tackle and every vulnerability I uncover brings me closer to becoming a well-rounded cybersecurity professional.

🛠️ Skills I'm Developing:
From penetration testing to bug bounties, I’m constantly expanding my technical expertise and sharpening my skillset to stay ahead in the field.

📢 Knowledge Sharing:
I believe in the power of community, diversity, and the exchange of ideas. Different perspectives strengthen our ability to solve complex problems, and that’s why I’m committed to sharing the tools, techniques, and insights I gather along the way. In cybersecurity, growth is best achieved together.

🌐 Why Cybersecurity?
The thrill of solving complex problems and the responsibility of protecting critical digital infrastructures drive me. Cybersecurity is a dynamic, ever-evolving field that challenges me daily, and I’m committed to continuously learning and adapting within it.

💼 What I’m Seeking:
I’m eager to apply my skills in a collaborative environment, tackle complex cybersecurity challenges, and contribute to impactful projects. I’m also excited about gaining hands-on experience and learning from seasoned professionals through mentorship.

📖 Follow My Learning Journey:
On my Medium profile, you can read about everything I’m learning, the CTF challenges I’ve solved, and key takeaways from my cybersecurity journey. I regularly share my experiences and the latest insights that I’ve gathered. Be sure to check out the link at the bottom of my page for more.
